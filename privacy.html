Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026 · Applies to the Resurface for X Chrome Extension

Short version: Resurface for X does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

1. Overview

Resurface for X ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension developed by mxxfun. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles information. By installing and using the Extension you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Collect

The Extension does not collect any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or account information
  • Browsing history or URLs visited
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

3. Data Stored Locally

The Extension stores the following data locally on your device only, using the Chrome storage.local API:

  • URLs and content of posts you bookmark on X (Twitter)
  • Timestamps and spaced-repetition scheduling metadata for those bookmarks
  • Your extension preferences (e.g. recall frequency, notification settings)

This data never leaves your browser. It is not sent to any external server, third party, or the developer.

4. Permissions Used

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions, each strictly necessary for core functionality:

  • storage — to save your bookmarks and settings locally in chrome.storage.local
  • alarms — to schedule periodic background reminders about forgotten bookmarks
  • notifications — to send push reminders when a bookmark is due for review
  • host permission for x.com / twitter.com — to detect when you bookmark a post and to inject ghost cards into your timeline

No permission is used to collect, read, or transmit personal data.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, send data to, or load resources from any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The Extension communicates solely with x.com (to display post content that is already visible in your browser tab). No additional network requests are made.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any data — because we do not collect any data in the first place.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally-stored data (bookmarks and settings) is retained on your device until you explicitly delete it. You can remove all stored data at any time via the Extension's Settings panel → "Clear all bookmarks".

Uninstalling the Extension from Chrome will also remove all locally-stored data associated with it.

8. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, this applies equally to all age groups.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
